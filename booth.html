            Mobile and solutions journalism: Shooting the stories

            Dougal Shaw

            Dougal Shaw is the BBC's only Video Innovation Journalist, working within the BBC News Business Unit. As well as creating original digital reports himself, he coordinates digital content, including videos optimised for platforms like Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn. He was chosen as a founding member of the Video Innovation Lab which created the BBC's first video offerings for Instagram and Snapchat. He also helped to set up the successful strand BBC Trending.
